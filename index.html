Who we are:

The DevOps Club at Michigan Technological University is a registered student organization dedicated to providing resources to students and improving the quality and availability of information related to DevOps and DevOps practices, such as:

  • DevOps best practices in industry
  • Infastructure as Code
  • Continuous Integration
  • Continuous Delivery
  • Software Defined Infrastructure
  • Software Defined Networking
  • Voice over IP (VOIP)
  • And more!
